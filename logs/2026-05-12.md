## 35. Search Insert Position

- Topic: Binary Search
- Idea: Find the first index whose value is greater than or equal to `target`.
- Key point: Use a left-closed, right-open search range `[left, right)`.

## 70. Climbing Stairs

- Topic: Dynamic Programming
- Idea: The number of ways to reach step `n` equals ways to reach `n - 1` plus ways to reach `n - 2`.
- Key point: Only keep the previous two states to reduce space to `O(1)`.
